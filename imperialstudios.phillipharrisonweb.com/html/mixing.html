Mixing & Post-Production

Here at Imperial Studios we offer mixing and mastering services as the final process in an in-house production to make your work stand up shoulder to shoulder against commercial recordings. We also undertake mix only projects where the tracking may have been done elsewhere. Often a new pair of ears is beneficial and allows fresh perspectives to be explored.

With access to high end plug-ins from the likes of Universal Audio and Waves plus crystal clear AD/DA conversion we have everything on hand to ensure a commercial ready mix and master recording of which to be proud of.
