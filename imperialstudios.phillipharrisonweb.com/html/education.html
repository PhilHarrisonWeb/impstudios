Education

We are proud of the inclusive and inspiring environment that we have created. We encourage learning, discovery and creativity and it is our desire to nurture the next generation of musicians.

We offer one-to-one lessons on guitar, bass, drums and piano. The lessons mix practical study with the gentle introduction of theoretical concepts. We also encourage collaboration between musicians, ensemble performing and creation of music just for it's own sake!

Please contact us for more information on our music education programme.
